01 · The Journey

Not a career path. A convergence.

Two decades building the depth that sovereign capital advisory demands — from enterprise IT governance to giga-programme delivery to digital twin deployment.

Started as an engineer — MSc Management Engineering, Politecnico di Milano. Foundation built in enterprise architecture, programme governance, and IT strategy across Italy and Australia. That rigour in systems thinking is the difference between advisory that survives contact with delivery and advisory that doesn't.

A decade in the GCC — Accenture, then Deloitte Australia, then back — sharpened that foundation into a specific capability: leading capital programme transformation at the intersection of governance architecture, PMIS strategy, and digital intelligence. Not as a generalist. As someone who has personally operated every layer of the delivery stack on sovereign programmes.

The last three years as Director at Accenture Industry X are the culmination — leading multi-billion-dollar sovereign capital portfolios across UAE, KSA, and Qatar at the level where strategic advisory authority and technical delivery credibility must coexist.
